## [0.2.0] - 2024-12-08

### Added - Season 2: Training Day (NES Era)

#### Gameplay
- Side-scrolling action platformer engine
- 4 playable stages with increasing difficulty
- Character switching system (press C)
- Boss battle against the Commander
- Enemy variety: dummies, drones, turrets, soldiers, elites

#### Characters
- Natasha: Electric projectile attack (mid-range)
- Maria: Melee combat (high speed, close range)
- Anya: Ranged bullet shots (long range, precision)
- Olga: Tactical drone strikes (area control)

#### Narrative
- Full cutscene dialog system
- Story progression through Episodes 2-4
- Cliffhanger setup for Season 3

#### Technical
- NES-accurate 256×224 resolution (3x scaled)
- Full 2C02 PPU color palette
- Multi-voice chiptune audio
- Parallax scrolling backgrounds
- Particle effects system
- Camera follow with smooth interpolation

#### UI/UX
- Health and energy bars
- Lives counter
- Score tracking
- Stage progress indicator
- Boss health bar
- Touch-friendly control overlay

## [0.1.0] - 2024-12-08

### Added - Season 1: The Awakening (Atari Era)

#### Gameplay
- 4 single-screen missions (one per character)
- Character-specific mechanics:
  - Natasha: Maze navigation, node decryption
  - Maria: Arena combat, enemy neutralization
  - Anya: Fixed-position shooting, target destruction
  - Olga: Stealth/collection, patrol avoidance

#### Technical
- Atari 2600-accurate 160×120 resolution (4x scaled)
- Authentic NTSC color palette (128 colors)
- CRT shader effects (scanlines, phosphor glow, screen curvature, flicker)
- Web Audio API chiptune synthesis
- Pixel-perfect rendering

#### UI/UX
- Title screen with character selection
- Score tracking
- Progress dots for screen completion
- Mission-specific objectives display
